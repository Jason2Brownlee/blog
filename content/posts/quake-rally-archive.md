I've developed some new Quake archive projects.

The first is a Quake Rally archive:

* [Quake Rally Archive](https://github.com/Jason2Brownlee/QuakeRallyArchive)

This includes all releases of Quake Rally, all third-party maps, and even precursor mods and extension mods.

One of the precursor mods was "Quake Kart" by Quake Command, an early quake mod group that later went on to work for Valve and work on Half-Life.

Very little of Quake Kart surrives and I found it fascinating. I ended up going deep and writing a research essay on it:

* [Quake Kart Release History](https://github.com/Jason2Brownlee/QuakeRallyArchive/blob/main/research/research_quake_kart.md)

I also attempted to elicit more info from the community:

* [Quake Kart (QKart) Mod Recollections?](https://www.reddit.com/r/quake/comments/1r9fq4r/quake_kart_qkart_mod_recollections/)

Anyway, this lead me to develop a Quake Command archive:

* [Quake Command Archive](https://github.com/Jason2Brownlee/QuakeCommandArchive)

This attempts to archive all release for all of their early Quake mods like throwing axe, flamethrower, aircraft and more.

Fun, fun!
